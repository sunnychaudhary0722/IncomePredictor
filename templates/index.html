Canada's GDP and Economic Overview

Canada, one of the world's largest economies, has seen consistent growth in its GDP over the years, driven by its vast natural resources, high-tech industries, and services sector. The per capita income, a key indicator of economic well-being, reflects the overall economic output divided by the population. This project predicts the per capita income for any given year based on historical data, helping to understand the future trajectory of Canada's economic growth.
